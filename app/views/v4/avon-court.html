Who is responsible for the building's safety

These are also known as accountable persons.

Greenways Limited

Greenways Limited is responsible for:

  • the external walls and roof
  • the routes that residents can walk through, like corridors, staircases and fire doors
  • updating the information held by the Building Safety Regulator

Greenways Limited is the principal accountable person. They`re responsible for registering the building and making sure the information we hold is updated.

They are based at 17 Rosewood Avenue, Oakshire, Manchester, M8 4JT.

Rune Ltd

Rune Ltd is responsible for:

  • maintaining plant and equipment - this includes lifts, firefighting equipment and any other machinery in the building

They are based at 56 Thistle Street, Heatherfield, EH3 6QW.

An individual

An individual is responsible for:

  • facilities that residents share, like laundry rooms or bin rooms

We cannot publish personal data, like the name or address, of individuals.

If you think any of this information is wrong

If any of this information has changed, it can take up to 10 weeks to show here.

If you are part of Greenways Limited

As the principal accountable person, you must tell the Building Safety Regulator if any of this information has changed. Find out more about making changes to your registration.

If you are a resident

If you live in Avon Court and think any of this information has changed, contact Greenways Limited. Tell them what you think has changed and ask them to change it. They should have shared their engagement strategy with all residents of this building. The engagement strategy should tell you how to complain or ask them questions.

I have tried and failed to contact Greenways Limited
If you have contacted Greenways Limited, but not had a response, contact the Building Safety Regulator. If they have not already told us about the change, we will contact them about it.
